Here's some great info about your favorite type of doggy

The Samoyed Name Is Often Mispronounced

Most people assume this handsome dog’s name is pronounced Sa-MOY-ed, but the correct pronunciation is actually SAMmy-ed. The Samoyed also goes by a few other names, including some real tongue-twisters: Bjelkier, Samoiedskaya Sobaka, and Nemetskaya Laika.

Nicknames for the breed include “Sammy,” “Sam,” and “Smiley.”

Samoyeds Are Good with Children and Other Dogs

A well-socialized Sammy loves kids, but it’s important that interactions with small children be supervised to prevent the dog from unintentionally knocking over a little guy or girl.

Samoyeds typically have calm temperaments, so they do well around other dogs – especially if they are raised together from an early age.
